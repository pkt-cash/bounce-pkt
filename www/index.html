bounce.pkt

Redirect a PKT domain to your classical domain.

How it works

  1. In PKT Dashboard add a CNAME record to your domain with Host = @ and Value = bounce.pkt
  2. Then add a TXT record with content: BOUNCE=https://<the website to redirect to>
    Example: BOUNCE=https://pkteerium.xyz
  3. Save changes on the dashboard
  4. Come back here and enter your PKT domain below and click "Update"
